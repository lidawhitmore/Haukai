Our Privacy Statement

In accordance with New Zealand Privacy Act 2020


We collect personal information from you, including information about your:


  • name
  • contact information
  • interactions with us
  • billing or purchase information

We collect your personal information in order to:


  • gather analytical information about how the website is used to allow us to improve the user experience

We keep your information safe by storing it at encrypted files and only allowing certain senior staff to access it .


We keep your information for 2 years at which point we securely destroy it by erasing all information we hold in relation to you.


You have the right to ask for a copy of any personal information we hold about you, and to ask for it to be corrected if you think it is wrong. If you’d like to ask for a copy of your information, or to have it corrected, please contact us at LudmilaWhitmore@myop.ac.nz.
